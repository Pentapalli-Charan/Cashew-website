Our Story

From Palasa's Farms to Your Table

Palasa, in Srikakulam district of Andhra Pradesh, is one of India's most celebrated cashew-growing regions. We are a small family-run business that brings the finest cashews directly from these farms to homes across Vizag.

By cutting out the middlemen, we ensure that you receive fresher cashews at fairer prices. Every order is carefully packed by hand and delivered straight to your doorstep with love.

We believe that the best things in life — like a handful of good cashews — should be accessible to every family.
